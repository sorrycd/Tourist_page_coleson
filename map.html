The Geography of Lethbridge

Lethbridge is located on the southern Canadian Prairies, characterized by rolling grasslands and coulees formed by the Oldman River. The city's landscape is marked by these steep-sided valleys, with the distant Rocky Mountains visible to the west. The region's climate is occasionally tempered by Chinook winds, providing milder winter days. These valleys contrast with the vast open prairie, while the looming silhouette of the Rocky Mountains can be seen to the west. The area around Lethbridge also features diverse ecosystems, from riverine habitats in the Oldman River valley to native grasslands on the surrounding plains, offering many exploration opportunities for nature enthusiasts.
