The People

Lethbridge represents a mix of indigenous communities, long-standing Canadian families, and newer immigrants. The presence of educational institutions adds a youthful vibe to the city. Rooted in coal mining and agriculture, today's residents work in diverse sectors. The community is characterized by its friendly demeanor, diversity, and a powerful sense of unity. With a history rooted in collaboration, we work together to support local businesses and foster cultural understanding, making the city a welcoming place for all.
